About

I create digital and physical experiences to connect brands with their audiences from products and services to everything in between. My work leverages principles of visual communication, interaction design, user experience, cognitive & behavioral psychology, and human-computer interaction.

Currently at R/GA Los Angeles as an Experience Design Director. My work has spanned from conversational interfaces and educational experiential activations for Samsung, wearable apps and systems platforms for Fossil, and websites for a variety of brands including Love Has No Labels for the Ad Council and Michael Kors. Other brands I have touched on include California Lottery, Google, Soul Cycle, Verizon, and the list continues to grow.

In my spare time I teach Interaction Design at SVA NYC, race drones with the Safety Third Racing team, and am building a lifestyle/drone side project, STUDS.
